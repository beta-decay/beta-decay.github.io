About


I'm Christian, a final year Physics with Astronomy student at Durham University and I am currently applying for PhDs in astrophysics. My academic interests lie in observation astronomy, particularly that of AGN and accretion onto complex objects.

On this website you can find various hobby projects of mine, implemented in JavaScript and HTML. Some of my favourites include the numerical solver of Friedmann's equations given your choice of cosmological parameters and the interpreter for the programming language I designed, Noether.

For more information about me, check out my LinkedIn or my Twitter.
